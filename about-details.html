About Me

Software Engineer & AI/ML Enthusiast!

Hello! I'm Pratik Rakhonde, a passionate Software Engineer and AI/ML enthusiast currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Dr. D.Y. Patil Institute of Management & Research, Pimpri. My journey into the world of technology began with a curiosity for solving real-world problems using code, and it has since evolved into a deep love for building scalable applications and exploring the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

My Journey

My fascination with technology started at a young age when I built my first website using HTML and CSS. Over the years, I've honed my skills in programming, web development, and AI/ML. My academic journey, from earning a Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA) to pursuing an MCA, has been filled with hands-on projects, internships, and a relentless drive to learn and innovate.

I've worked on diverse projects, from AI-powered chatbots to full-stack web applications, and contributed to open-source communities like Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC). Each experience has shaped my understanding of technology and reinforced my passion for creating impactful solutions.
